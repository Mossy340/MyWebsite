How to do a car oil change

  1. Prep and lift the car securely with jack stands or ramps.
  2. Draing the old oil completeley into a drain pan.
  3. Replace the oil filter with a new lubricated one.
  4. Refill the engine with fresh oil.
  5. Check the dipstick level and inspect for any leaks.

Core Internet Terms

IP Address
A unique string of number that acts like a home address for any device online.
DNS
A phonebook of the internet that turns webiste names into numbers that computers understand like and IP address.
URL
The web address you type into the browser to goto a specific page.
HTTP
The standard rules and system used for sending and loading webpages across the internet.
